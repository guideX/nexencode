Chorus (short delay, short depth, float rate, short waveform, short dry, short wet, short invert_feedback, short mixing, short feedback)

Aplies a chorus on the waveform.
Example: Chorus (35, 25, 2, 1, 75, 75, 1, -1, 0)
delay in ms, depth in ms, rate in Hz, waveform = 1: linear 0: sinus,
 dry: Part of the Original in Percent,
wet: Part of the chorus-wave in Percent,
invert_feedback: -1 or 1(default), mixing: 1 or -1 (default),
 feedback in Percent.

CFilter (short Filter) Looks for Clicks and removes them. With Factor you can specify the factor, the routine multiplies with the average difference between 2 points of the whole wave-File as a criterium for a click.

Distortion (short dry_out, short distorted_out, short threshold_level, short clamp_level, short gate)
Example: Distortion (1005, 560, 3, 0, 0)
dry_out: Original in Pecent, distorted_out: Distorted-wave in Percent, threshold_level in Percent, clamp_level in Percent, gate 0 or 1.

Echo (short delay, short ratio)
Aplies an echo on the waveform
Example: Echo (900, 90)
delay in ms, ratio = Percent of the Echo

Eq (short gain1, ..., short gain9, short filter_dry)

Multiplies the following frequency-Intervals with the in gain1 to gain9 specified Values, the Values varies from -100% to +100%, 0% is default and meens a regular distribution. Filterdry is the Part of the in Eq calculated Wavform in Percent.

gain1: 32 Hz, gain2: 80 Hz, gain3: 200 Hz, gain4: 400 Hz, gain5: 1 KHz, gain6: 2 KHz, gain7: 4 KHz, gain8: 8 KHz, gain9: 16 KHz

FadeIn (short Seconds10th)

Seconds10th meens the 10th part of a second
Starts to play with Volume 0 and brings the Volume within Seconds10th on the normal Volume.

Example FadeIn (50) In 5 Seconds after start from 0 to normal Volume

FadeOut (short Seconds10th) -> FadeIn
